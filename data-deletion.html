Data Deletion Instructions

This page explains how to delete your personal data from Interline, including information associated with Facebook Login.

1. Delete data directly from the Interline portal

If you have access to your Interline workspace, you (or an admin of the workspace) can delete certain data directly in the portal:

  • Your contact profile
  • Your message history
  • Notes, tags, and related metadata

Once deleted, this information will no longer be available inside the workspace.

2. Request full data deletion via email

If you want all personal data related to you removed from Interline’s systems, email our Privacy team:

Email: privacy@interline.chat

Please include:

  • Your full name
  • The email address or phone number used
  • (Optional) The business/workspace you interacted with

After confirming your identity, we will delete or anonymize all data held by Interline unless we must retain some information for legal or security reasons.

3. Remove Interline access from Facebook

If you used Facebook Login, you can also remove Interline from your Facebook account:

  1. Open Facebook
  2. Go to Settings » Apps and Websites
  3. Select Interline
  4. Click Remove

This only removes Facebook access. To delete data stored with Interline, follow steps 1 or 2 above.
