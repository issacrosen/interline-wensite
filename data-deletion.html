Data Deletion Instructions

1. Delete data directly through Interline

If you have access to an Interline workspace, you or the workspace admin can delete your data directly in the portal. This includes:

  • Your contact profile
  • Message history
  • Tags, notes, and metadata associated with your account

Once removed, this information will no longer appear inside the workspace.

2. Request complete data deletion via email

To request deletion of all personal data stored by Interline, please contact:

Email: privacy@interline.chat

Please include:

  • Your full name
  • The email address or phone number used
  • (Optional) The business or workspace you interacted with

After verifying your identity, we will delete or anonymize all personal data Interline manages unless required for legal or security purposes.

3. Remove Interline access from Facebook

If you signed in using Facebook Login:

  1. Open Facebook
  2. Go to Settings → Apps and Websites
  3. Select Interline
  4. Click Remove

This disables future access to Facebook data. To delete data inside Interline, use steps 1 or 2 above.
